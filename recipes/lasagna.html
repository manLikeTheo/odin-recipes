Lasagna

Layered with a rich meat sauce and a creamy parmesan white sauce, plus the perfect amount of mozzarella cheese.

Wanna Make Lasagna? Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • White sauce
  • Torn mozarella

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to about 200deg/gas6C/400degF and remember to cover the lasagna with a thin foil forf the first thirty minutes
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce in the bottom of baking dish
  3. Add a single layer of pasta sheets
  4. Add a layer of white sauce followed by another single layer of pasta sheets
  5. Spread an even layer of meat sauce
  6. Finish off the process with a layer of tomato based sauce or white sauce and grate over plenty of torn mozarella
  7. Remove the thin foil in the last 10-20minutes of baking and bake until the top is golden or crispy and the whole dish is piping hot through
