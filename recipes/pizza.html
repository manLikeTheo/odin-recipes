Hummus Pizza

Unique pizza for those bored with the traditional pizza. You can top with your favorite veggies and cheese

Wanna Make a good delicious hummus pizza? Start with a solid dough

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce can refridgerated pizza crust dough(thaw, if frozen)
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 1 and half cups sliced bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup brocoli florets
  • 2 cups shredded monterey jack cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to about 220 degrees C
  2. Roll out pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus, and top with shredded cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve.
